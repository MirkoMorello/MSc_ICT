The following file contains the pinout used for connecting the SiPeed field microphones array to the Raspberry Pi. The the hardware point of view, the mics needs to be connected this way:

LED_CK ---> GPIO 11 (PIN 23)
LED_DA ---> GPIO 10 (PIN 19)
MIC_D0 ---> GPIO 21 (PIN 40)
MIC_D1 ---> GPIO 20 (PIN 38)
MIC_D2 ---> GPIO 26 (PIN 37)
MIC_D3 ---> GPIO 16 (PIN 36)
MIC_WS ---> GPIO 19 (PIN 35)
MIC_CK ---> GPIO 18 (PIN 12)
VIN ------> 3.3VDC (PIN 1)
GND ------> GROUND (PIN 6)
